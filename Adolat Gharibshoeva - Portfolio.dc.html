About Me

I'm Adolat Gharibshoeva, a Computer Science junior (Class of 2028) at the University of Central Asia in Naryn.

I grew up in Khorog, in the Pamir Mountains of GBAO, where limited access to opportunity shaped everything I build. My work lives at the intersection of technology, AI and education: I want the tools that opened doors for me to reach the rural and mountain communities that rarely see them.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me in Persian poetry and Hosseini's novels, behind a camera in the mountains, or lost in music — the arts and the sciences have never felt separate to me.
